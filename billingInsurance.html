Helping All Patients Receive Quality Care

Mercy Health offers you a variety of financial services, including several options for paying your bill, financial assistance and online requests for estimated out-of-pocket costs. We also can assist with questions about or signing up for the Affordable Care Act, Health Insurance Marketplace or Expanded Medicaid.
For more information on billing or financial services, please call our customer service line at 1-800-494-5797.
