Career Development Program

R.I.S.E. Up Program
Reach out. Investigate. Seak. Elivate

The R.I.S.E. Up Program utilizes a 7-Step Career Pathway Process:

  • Intake – Orientation and registration into the program
  • Career Coaching – One-on-one sessions with a career coach
  • Support – Funding for training and financial support, if needed
  • Learning – Education, training, and work-based learning
  • Credentials – Certificates, degrees, certifications and licenses
  • Profiles – Evidence-based career profiles
  • Placement – Referral of qualified candidates
