Library Services

Patients and Provider Resourses

For patients, the library is staffed with health information professionals who can assist you in understanding your health and wellness concerns. Resources include books, pamphlets, and many online resources which are presented in plain language and free of medical jargon, and can be borrowed at no cost for extended periods of time. The library is badge in only and located next to the Lab on Wege Floor 2. Please contact the librarian to make an appointment.

For more information, or for a one-on-one information consult, please reach out to Caralee Witteveen-Lane at caralee.witteveen-lane@fortisCare.com or 616-685-6243.
